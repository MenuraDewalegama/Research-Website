Literature Survey

Following section is to comprehensively review current trends and projects that other researchers have done to overcome issues with the taxi service industry. Since the study that was carried out is to address the main four problems in taxi services the literature review also based on those factors as below.
• Emotion-based music player
• Driver distraction detection
• Forgotten items detection
• Driver vocal misbehavior detection

There are several methods that have been proposed to recognize human emotion using facial expressions. But some of those methods use additional hardware resources like sensors or EEG (electroencephalogram) to generate results. In [1], the invented BCI-based mobile multimedia controller was mainly focused on capturing the cognitive state of the mind. To do that EEG was used to record the electrical activity of the brain’s neurons. But the main drawback of that innovation is the usage of such hardware components which may require a laboratory environment.

Many researchers have done some development to detect driver distraction. In many research studies, they have defined that driver distraction varies considering factors such as drowsiness, eye movement, and the behavior of the driver. There are mainly two approaches to develop a driver distraction detection system either using sensors or using high deep learning algorithms. The paper described in [2] explicitly reviewed a few of the available methods and behavioral features that can be extracted to follow up when conducting research like driver distraction. Such as Viola-Jones used to detect eye state and blinking rate and SVM used to detect yawning.

In object detection systems, there are several industrial applications available to ease day-to-day life such as Google Lens which was created by Google and used Neural Network algorithm to develop the application. And it is use mobile camera to detect objects [3]. In the field of vocal misbehavior, there are similar studies has been done over the past few years. In [4], authors created a system called BeyondVerbal which can capture human voices and extract human attitudes. The application was built as an API, and it was hosted in a cloud as well.

Research Problem & Solution

Research Problem

There are number of doubts regarding taxi services in the world. We have focused on some major issues in our research and going to address them. Mainly, as customers we get into a taxi vehicle, we are not aware of the driver. Also, we do not know whether the driver's driving is safe or not. Even the vehicle owner or the management of taxi service does not know whether the driver is doing his job according to the correct standards. Currently, they do not have a method to monitor the driver or to get to know about the driver’s behavior during the driving apart from the customer reviews. These days, the common mistakes that drivers use to do are, making calls during the drive, not focusing on the road, looking around and not concentrating on the driving, another common fault done by the driver is the way they talk to their customers. So, these are some real-world problems we detected in this area of taxi service.

Apart from that, we have witnessed that some drivers are playing songs in their vehicles which are not pleasant for our ears. Especially in some emotional stages. Let’s say a customer gets into a taxi car to go to a funeral, but he/she gets to hear a pop song. So that is not helpful for the customer. Customer might ask the driver to switch the song or to stop the music. So that is a problem we are addressing in our Research project. Also, some common mistake we all suffer is forgetting something inside the Taxi. The proposed system will address that issue as well.




Research Solution


Research Objectives

Emotion Based Music Player

Detect passengers’ emotions by monitoring their facial expressions and playing music according to their current emotions. So that customers can enjoy their trips by listening to music that they like at the moment.

Driver Distraction Detector

Track whether the driver is concentrating on the road or not. If the driver is not concentrating, the system alerts them. So that the customers and the taxi service companies can have well-disciplined drivers.

Forgotten Items Detector

Once a customer leaves, the detector will identify any forgotten items on the seat of the vehicle. If there is anything then the system will notify the driver in real-time. As a result of that taxi service gets a good reputation and passengers won’t miss their belongings.

Driver Vocal Misbehavior Detector

Detect the drivers’ voice tone and get their emotional state when they communicate with their customers. If the drivers’ communication manner is not proper, then the taxi service can get actions on them.

Methodology

Overall System Diagram

As per the figure, the proposed system consists of three main components. The first component represents the IoT devices including two cameras, a microphone, and an android tablet. One camera is directed at the driver and the other camera is focused on the back seat of the vehicle which will be used to capture the passenger’s emotion and forgotten items which can be left on the seat. The microphone is mounted near the steering wheel to capture the driver’s voice. These devices feed the required input to the system. The second component is the backend cloud server which connects the models to the android infotainment system. It acts as the brain of the system as it will return all the predictions according to the inputs it gets. And the last component is the smart infotainment application. It contains all the functionalities of a car infotainment system. The smart infotainment system acts as the mediator between IoT devices and the cloud server.
